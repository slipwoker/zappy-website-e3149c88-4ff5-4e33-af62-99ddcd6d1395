Frequently Asked Questions

We're here to support your journey with Rooted in Love. Below are answers to common questions about collaborations, timelines, and how we work together.

Can I request custom designs or personalized products?

Absolutely! We love creating personalized pieces that honor your unique motherhood story. Reach out through our contact form with your vision, and we'll explore how we can bring it to life together.

Do you collaborate with other creators or brands?

Yes, we welcome collaborations that align with our values of sacred motherhood, creativity, and gentle parenting. If you have a collaboration idea, share your proposal and we'll see if it's a beautiful fit.

What are your typical project timelines?

Custom projects typically take 2-4 weeks from initial consultation to completion, depending on complexity. We'll provide a clear timeline once we understand your needs and ensure every detail receives the attention it deserves.

What information should I include when reaching out?

Share as much or as little as feels right—your project vision, timeline, budget range, and what drew you to Rooted in Love. The more we understand your story, the better we can serve your creative needs.

How do you handle content usage and creative rights?

All custom work is created with love and intention. We'll discuss usage rights during our consultation to ensure clarity and mutual respect. Our free printables are yours to enjoy personally, while commissioned pieces follow agreed-upon terms.

We're Here to Listen
