A Sacred Space for Motherhood

Rooted in Love was born from the beautiful chaos of raising two souls—a space where motherhood is honored as both gentle and fierce, where creativity flows through everyday moments, and where indigenous wisdom guides our path forward.

Gentle strength, rooted daily.

Here you'll find handcrafted pieces that celebrate the sacred journey of mothering: botanical illustrations that mirror nature's patience, feather line art that speaks to lightness and protection, and strawberry vines that remind us growth is both wild and intentional.

This is your peaceful escape—a place to breathe, create, and remember that raising children with love and intention is the most sacred work we do.
