A Journey Rooted in Love

This brand began in the quiet moments between chaos—when motherhood felt both sacred and overwhelming. It's a space where healing meets creativity, where we celebrate the beautiful complexity of raising children with intention and love.

Created by Anastacia, a mother of two, Rooted in Love emerged from a deep desire to honor the indigenous wisdom of nurturing sacred children. Each design, each illustration, carries the gentle strength of mothers who choose love-centered parenting even when the world feels heavy.

Through soft botanical art, delicate feather motifs, and hand-drawn strawberry vines, this brand offers a peaceful escape—a reminder that motherhood is both an ancient practice and a daily act of courage. Here, we root ourselves in what matters most: connection, healing, and the profound beauty of raising the next generation with grace.

A Mother's Journey to Sacred Roots

It started in the quiet hours—those moments between midnight feedings and dawn light, when the world felt impossibly still and my heart impossibly full. Motherhood cracked me open in ways I never expected, revealing wounds I didn't know existed and strength I never knew I had.

Through the sleepless nights and tender mornings, I discovered that healing isn't linear. It's woven into the everyday—the gentle rhythm of rocking a baby to sleep, the sacred pause before answering a toddler's question, the intentional choice to parent from love rather than fear. Each small moment became a thread in a larger tapestry of transformation.

Creativity became my anchor. In the margins of motherhood, I found myself drawing feathers and botanical forms, creating spaces that honored both the chaos and the beauty. What began as personal healing evolved into something I felt called to share—a gentle invitation for other mothers to root their days in love, to find the sacred in the ordinary, to raise their children with intention and grace.

Make the everyday sacred.

Our Guiding Values

These core principles shape everything we create at Rooted in Love—from the stories we share to the products we craft. Each value reflects our commitment to nurturing sacred motherhood with intention, creativity, and gentle strength.

Rooted Love

Every creation begins with love—the kind that grounds us in our purpose and connects us to our children, our heritage, and the earth beneath our feet. We believe motherhood is a sacred journey rooted in unconditional love and deep connection.

Gentle Strength

True strength doesn't need to be loud. It lives in the quiet moments of patience, the tender acts of care, and the resilience we carry as mothers. We honor the soft power that sustains families and nurtures generations.

Sacred Creativity

Creativity is our birthright and our healing practice. Through art, storytelling, and handmade beauty, we reclaim our voices and celebrate the divine feminine. Every brushstroke and word becomes an act of sacred expression.

How We Create Together

Every piece begins with intention and unfolds through connection. From the first spark of an idea to the moment it finds its home, each step is rooted in love, care, and the sacred journey of motherhood.

Discover

We begin by listening—to your story, your needs, and the quiet whispers of what your heart is calling for. This is where inspiration takes root, where we explore themes of motherhood, healing, and sacred connection.

Root

Ideas settle and deepen. We gather inspiration from indigenous wisdom, botanical beauty, and the tender strength of motherhood. This phase is about grounding the vision in authenticity and intention.

Create

With gentle hands and focused heart, we bring the vision to life. Whether it's hand-drawn illustrations, thoughtful product design, or curated creative resources, every detail is crafted with care and reverence for the motherhood journey.

Share

The creation finds its way to you—a gentle offering meant to support, inspire, and celebrate the beautiful chaos of raising sacred children. This is where our work becomes part of your story, rooted in love and shared with purpose.
